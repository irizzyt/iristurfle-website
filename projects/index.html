Economic Tracker

Economic indicators monitoring system for crisis detection and predictive analysis.

This project tracks key macroeconomic indicators including GDP growth, unemployment rates, inflation, and market volatility. It uses algorithmic analysis to identify patterns and early warning signals of potential economic crises, combining data analysis with economic theory to create predictive insights.

Key Features

  • Tracks key macroeconomic indicators including GDP growth, unemployment rates, inflation, and market volatility
  • Uses algorithmic analysis to identify patterns and early warning signals of potential economic crises
  • Implements threshold-based detection algorithms that flag when indicators exceed critical levels or show abnormal trends
  • Combines data analysis with economic theory from coursework to create predictive insights
  • Visualizes indicator relationships and trends to help understand economic stability and risk factors
